Manual Lines, Still in Userland.

The left panel is ordinary browser flow. The right panel uses layoutWithLines() to place every line manually, while keeping the same wrapped text and height estimate. Resize it, click any line, and inspect the boundary cursors that fall out of the engine.

North Star

Height estimation stays cheap. Rich line data stays explicit. That opens the door to resize-aware editorial layouts, anchored reflow, and text choreography without handing control back to opaque browser block layout.
